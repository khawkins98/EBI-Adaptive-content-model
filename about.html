About this visulisation

This is a forest-to-trees gateway visulisation that will, once finished, give an overview of the structure of the EMBL-EBI website and give indicators to which areas of the site are performing well.

    Key components
  • Visulisation of how Core Content areas connect (/about, /services, /research, etc.)
  • Common inward and outward areas (newsletters, google, etc.)
  • Which user types are targeted by the content of various Core Content areas
  • Links to the Core Content Spec
  • Metrics via Google Analytics of which areas are performing well
